About Our School

Zilla Parishad High School (ZPHS) Challapalli is a co-educational, government-run school located in the Krishna district. Our mission is to provide quality education to our students.

Address: Challapalli, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh

Contact: 123-456-7890

Academics

We offer classes from 6th to 10th standard. Our academic programs are designed to foster knowledge, skills, and values in a holistic manner.

  • Class 6 - Class 10
  • Subjects: Math, Science, Social Studies, Telugu, English, and more
  • Extra-Curricular: Sports, Arts, and Cultural Activities
